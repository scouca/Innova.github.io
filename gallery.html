Lima's bollboard-purifier

Lima's bollboard-purifierAdvertising billboard that gives access to drinking water We got contacted by the department of engineering at the university of Lima in Peru. While their air is very humid they only have 2,5 cm of rain every year. They had an idea where they would put up a giant advertising billboard that absorbs humid air and converts it in to purified water, generating more then 90 liters per day. They contacted us with the idea and had a small demonstration prototype where they showed how it would work. They did not have the resources to put up one in the scale they needed. And even less so more then one. To make this billboard possible they first had to contact the city department and ask for permission, seeing as the idea is the best solution to get water in the town it got granted Then they made a list of requirements necessary to complete the billboard. Ranging from material to help with finding the most practical solutions. We helped them to get funds for the giant billboard and also sent the idea to the universities we are in touch with to see if they could help with finding solutions.


Mansukh Prajapati.

In India there is a guy who had an idea for a fridge that uses no electricity. Mansukh Prajapati. The fridge is made out of clay and can keep fruits and vegetables fresh for many days. Mr Prajapati came to us because he wanted to spread this first and formost over India and eventually the world. The invention is fairly simple but together with him we had to find a solution that would benefit as many people as possible with as little material as possible. We also helped him with funding for making the idea spread.
